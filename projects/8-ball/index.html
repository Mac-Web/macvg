8 Ball Pool
Famobi

About

8 Ball Pool is a fun pool simlation game where you try to hit as many balls as you can into the 6 holes along on the sides of the table. Use your mouse to aim and drag bag to determine how much force to apply. Be very careful and accurate so that the angle and the speed can hit the ball into the holes! Always hit a ball by hitting the white ball and letting it bounce on another ball, but never hit the white ball in one of the holes! Enjoy!
