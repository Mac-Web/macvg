circloO 2
Florian van Strien

About

Just like circloO but enhanced, circloO 2 is an exciting and addicting fun game where you need to move the ball across different obstacles and balls in the many different levels. Each time your ball reaches the circle, the map expands and more obstacles are created. After you reached every circle, the level's over and you can move on to the next level with more interesting obstables. Jump, fall, and roll the ball in different places stratigically to reach the goal!

Controls

  • Arrow / WASD Keys: Move
  • Esc / Back: Pause
