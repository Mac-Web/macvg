Endless Truck
Famobi

About

In this fun truck driving game, you need to control your car to complete the missions of the level, while driving through obstacles and keeping fuel in check. Each level has three missions, and to beat the level, you need to complete all of them in one run. Tilt left or tilt right to perform flips in midair, and adjust the truck's balance to land perfectly and receive a boost! There are fuel boxes that recharge your fuel. Don't miss them or you'll run out of gas! Jump to get over obstacles. Every time you do a stunt or collect them during the drive, you earn cash which you can use to upgrade your truck and drive even longer and beat your personal record!

Controls

  • Left Arrow: Tilt Left
  • Right Arrow: Tilt Right
  • Space: Jump
